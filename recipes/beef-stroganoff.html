Simple Beef Stroganoff

Description

10 minutes of prep time, then 6 hours of doing whatever you want. Quick, easy, and delicious.

Ingredients

  • 1x (10.75 ounce) can of condensed cream of mushroom soup
  • 1x (1 ounce) package of dry onion soup mix
  • 1 pound of cubed beef stew meat
  • 4 ounces of cream cheese
  • 1/2 a cup of water, or more as needed

Steps

  1. Whisk cream of mushroom soup, water, and dry onion soup in a slow cooker.
  2. Stir meat into the soup mixture.
  3. Cover and cook on Low until beef is tender in 6 to 8 hours. Alternatively, cook on High for 4 hours. Be sure to test for doneness after 2 hours.
  4. Remove from heat and stir cream cheese into beef until smooth.
